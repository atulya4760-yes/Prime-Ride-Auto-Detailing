About Us

Prime Ride Auto Detailing was built with a simple goal: make it easier for busy drivers in the GTA to keep their vehicles clean without wasting time at crowded car washes.

We’re a young, hardworking team focused on building something real from the ground up. What started as a small idea quickly became a service built around convenience, attention to detail, and treating every vehicle with care.

Whether it’s a daily driver, family SUV, or weekend car, our goal is the same every time: show up on time, make the process easy, and leave the vehicle looking refreshed inside and out.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does car detailing usually cost?

Pricing depends on the size of the vehicle, the condition of the interior and exterior, and any add-on services like pet hair removal or stain treatment. Our base services start from the prices listed above, and final quotes are confirmed after we review your details.

Do you come to my home or workplace?

Yes. We operate as a mobile service and work throughout Toronto and the GTA. When you request a quote, let us know your location and we’ll confirm availability.

How long does a detail take?

Most jobs take between 1.5 and 3.5 hours depending on the package, the size of the vehicle, and how much cleaning is needed.

Can I send photos before booking?

Yes. That actually helps us a lot. You can upload photos through the quote form so we can give you a more accurate estimate.

Do you clean very dirty interiors?

Yes, but heavily soiled vehicles, pet hair, deep stains, or strong odours may require extra time and additional pricing. The more detail you give us upfront, the more accurate the quote will be.

What extra services can I ask for?

Common requests include pet hair removal, salt cleanup, spot treatment for stains, and deeper interior attention for high-traffic vehicles. If you need something specific, mention it in the notes section of the form.
